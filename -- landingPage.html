START YOUR JOURNEY WITH PYRA

CONNECT YOUR WALLET
Securely link your Web3 wallet to access the Pyra network and verify your identity. This step ensures a seamless and secure connection to our decentralized ecosystem.

ACTIVATE YOUR NODE
Set up and deploy your node to start contributing to the network. Each active node strengthens the system, enhances security, and plays a vital role in maintaining the integrity of the decentralized structure.

Get rewarded for your participation! The more stable and active your node, the greater your earning potential. Support the Pyra ecosystem and receive rewards for helping build a decentralized future.
